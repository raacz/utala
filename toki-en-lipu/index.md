# utala musi pi lipu suli pi toki lili #MAMTW/#LTW/#LLL


jan Lakuse li toki wawa e ni tawa jan ale pi ma pona: o pana e lipu sina e toki lili sina tawa mi a! lipu en toki o toki pona e ijo pi lon ala. ona o musi kin. jan pi ma pona li pana! jan pi ma pona ala li pana! [sina ken lukin e ale pi toki wile ona lon ni](lawa.md).

kulupu li pana e toki lili  <abbr title="16">LLLW</abbr>!

kulupu li pana e lipu suli <abbr title="13">LLTW</abbr>!

jan Tepo en jan Lakuse li pana e ona ale tawa lipu ni 'utala.pona.la' la kulupu li ken lukin e lipu. 

sina wile la o lukin [nasin pi pana pilin](pana-pilin.md).

## suno pi toki pona

suno pi toki pona la jan Lakuse li toki e pona pi musi ale li kama e ni: jan li ken kalama e lipu ona e toki musi ona. taso pakala ilo li kama. jan mute li ken ala kalama pona. 

[sina ken kute e toki ona pi suno pi toki pona kepeken ilo Jutu](https://youtu.be/i56PDASYRtI?t=3251). kin la sina ken lukin e ni lon anpa: jan Lakuse li pana e sitelen seme. sina wile la, [o lukin e sitelen pi jan Lakuse kepeken nasin pi nasa ala](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1dCipAOWfboehRvqzvP2VS-KwX_ptdK2N1yd70LMWUao/edit?usp=sharing).
